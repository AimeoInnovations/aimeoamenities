Aimeo Amenities - India's Largest Supplier of Premium Guest Amenities !

Founded by hospitality veterans, Aimeo Guest Amenities is the leading supplier for all types of budget, luxury and eco-friendly range of guest amenities and toiletries. With special attention towards hygiene and quality standards, you are sure to receive top notch international quality products. With our manufacturing units spread across Uttarakhand (India), a warehouse in the National Capital Region and deep integration with DHL, Bluedart and FedEx, exports are smooth as we offer prompt delivery across the globe.

All our products are paraben free, cruelty free & 100% Vegan, free from artificial preservatives, dyes, colours, synthetic fragrances and animal by-products. Over 600 partners trust us for all their amenity requirements for over 8 years as we believe in building relations and the business follows. The company has a complete and diverse range of guest amenities and toiletries that it supplies to some reputed brands in India and exports to over 30 countries globally.
