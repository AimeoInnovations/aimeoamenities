Partner with Aimeo Amenities

If you’re looking for guest amenities and toiletries in bulk, Aimeo Amenities is a premier supplier based in Delhi NCR, India. They specialize in luxury bathroom amenities curated to enhance guests’ stays at hotels, resorts, airlines, cruise and hospitals.

Collections: Aimeo Amenities offers an elegant collection of spa-grade toiletries, including shampoos, conditioners, hand and body lotions, bath and shower gels, and guest soaps. These collections are thoughtfully packaged to complement various design styles and decors in personal units or are available as refill packs for installed dispensers.

Dry amenities: In addition to the collections, they provide an exhaustive collection of dry amenities as required by the client in a standard or custom packaging.

Sundry Items: Aimeo Amenities also offers sundry items such as makeup remover wipes, lip balm, slippers, shower caps, toothbrushes, and other personal care essentials.

Low Minimum Orders: They stand out by offering low minimum orders, making it convenient for properties of different sizes.

Volume Pricing: Further discounts may be available on bulk orders for hotels or properties.

Whether you’re managing a hotel, resort, hospital or manage procurement for an airline or cruise, Aimeo Amenities aims to be your partner in creating memorable guest experiences. Feel free to explore their offerings and find the perfect amenities for your business!
